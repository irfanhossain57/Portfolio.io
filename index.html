Research & Projects

Optimising Margin-Based Portfolio Management through Deep Reinforcement Learning
Undergraduate Thesis

Engineered a Transformer-based DRL framework using Multi-Head Attention (8–16 heads) within a PPO algorithm to perform temporal and cross-asset feature extraction for diversified trading strategies. Integrated realistic margin-trading constraints, including dynamic leverage ratios and maintenance requirements, into a custom environment with a multi-objective reward function balancing profit and risk. Achieved superior backtested performance on Dow 30 historical data (2009–2020), delivering higher risk-adjusted returns and reduced drawdowns compared to baseline methods.

Night Vision: Footage Enhancement and Colorization using Hierarchical Transformer

Designed a hierarchical Transformer model combining a ViT-32 encoder with a progressive deconvolution decoder to enhance and colorize low-light imagery while preserving semantic accuracy. Curated a 40,000-image dataset and developed a composite loss function to balance perceptual quality, color consistency, and edge preservation. Achieved a superior colorfulness score of 39.33.
